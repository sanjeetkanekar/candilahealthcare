About us

Under the leadership of Mr. Ganu G. Tari as a chief promoter of the society 11 members Subscribed Rs.1100/- as share capital and Ent.fee of Rs.110/-. for registration Cadila Healthcare Employees Cooperative Credit Society Ltd , Kundaim Goa.

The list of the promoter members is as below:-

  • Shri Ganu G. Tari - Chief Promoter
  • Shri Prashant V. Gawade - Member
  • Shri Suhas L. Natekar - Member
  • Shri Savesh D. Abhisheki - Member
  • Shri Shaba G. Gawas - Member
  • Shri Shamma S. Malik - Member
  • Shri Mahesh C. Kamat - Member
  • Shri Tukaram K. Borkar - Member
  • Shri Sudaiv P. Fadte - Member
  • Shri Subodh P. Mhamal - Member

The term of the Managing Committee has been five years with consisting strength of 11 members in the Managing Committee of the Society. As per the Goa Co-operative Societies Act 2001 & Rules 2003 the strength of Board of Directors shall be 11 members of which Two seats will be reserved for women and one seat for the members from SC or ST elected in accordance with provisions of Act Rules and bye-laws of the society to give more opportunity to the members to represent on the Board.

Any confirmed employee of Cadila Healthcare Limited/Zydus Lifesciences Limited at kudaim , Goa as specified under by-law No.4 (14) can avail membership of the society in a prescribed form by subscribing Entrance fee of Rs.10/- and at least one fully paid up share of Rs.100/-. Further he /she shall contribute to the society a minimum monthly cumulative deposit of Rs.1500/- till he continues to be a member of the society however, the General Body may increase and fixed the limit of monthly Cumulative deposit from time to time.
