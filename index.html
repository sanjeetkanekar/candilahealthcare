About us

The Cadila Healthcare Employees Cooperative Credit Society Ltd., was registered on 10-12-2018 under the registration Code No. ARCS/PZ/Credit/10(a)/65/Goa

It has been classified as “Credit Resources Society” under Sub- Classification 8(a) of rule 9(1) of the Co-operative Societies Rules, 1962 as applied to the State of Goa (Repealed as per Section 128 of Goa Co. op. Societies Act,2001) .Office of the Assistant Registrar of Cooperative Societies, Ponda Zone, Ponda ,Goa on 10-12-2018.

Objectives of the society

To encourage the habit of saving among its members by mobilizing regular monthly Cumulative deposits / Recurring Deposits and Fixed Deposits.

To provide Financial Assistance in the form of loan to needy members for all reasonable purpose on reasonable terms & conditions.

To provide education to its members, officers and employees on the principles of Co- operation to ensure healthy growth of Co-operative.

To undertake any other activities which are conductive and necessary to attain its objects.
